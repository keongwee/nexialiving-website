Three problems. One opportunity.
(01)

Urbanisation outpacing supply

Market reality

50M+ consumers, zero suitable housing. Wellness living, student accommodation and serviced apartments don't exist at scale.

Your opportunity

The living model is uniquely positioned to solve this at scale. With 400M+ people now living in Asian cities and demand for desirable places accelerating, we see a multitude of opportunities to access strong-growth markets.

(02)

Consumer expectations shifted

Market reality

Millennials and Gen Z demand community, wellness programming, flexibility and service — not just a room.

Your opportunity

Build an integrated lifestyle platform spanning wellness, student and serviced living to drive recurring demand and operational synergies with diversified revenue streams.

(03)

No clear playbook yet

Market reality

The wellness, student accommodation and serviced living sectors remain fragmented.

Your opportunity

Build a scalable, multi-segment platform that creates resilient assets and long-term investment growth.

Why work with Nexia

Scale premium living opportunities across multiple markets and segments with integrated technology and operations know-how. We remove the operational barrier to entry — so we can capitalise on Asia's living growth now.

The result: lower capex per unit, faster ramp to profitability, and portfolio defensibility.
  • Plug-and-play platform for rapid property launch
  • Cross-segment operating playbook — student to wellness to serviced apartments
  • Resident experience and community management that drives premium pricing
  • Benchmarked performance across the portfolio to de-risk early scaling
