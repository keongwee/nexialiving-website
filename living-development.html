Design & Development

Designing better living,
creating lasting value

Our design and development philosophy is rooted in the belief that design is the language of living and well-being. Every project follows a design-led approach that creates healthier, more engaging environments while delivering cost efficiencies, optimised space planning and inspiring contemporary living experiences.

Embracing flexible development strategies, we specialise in conversions, adaptive reuse and new-build projects that unlock value and extend asset longevity. Sales & Distribution

Connecting markets, partnerships & customers for sustainable growth

We connect global, regional and local markets to the right customers — combining a dedicated sales force, integrated omnichannel marketing and strategic alliances to build demand, visibility and long-term growth.

Three integrated capabilities

Dedicated Sales Force

We strategically target guests across global, regional and local markets and every business segment. Expert sales teams give B2B customers streamlined solutions — leveraging our scale, distribution channels and personalised service through a single point of contact that builds long-standing trust.

Omnichannel Marketing

An integrated strategy connecting digital and physical touchpoints — managing third-party distribution, driving social engagement, building brand partnerships and activating events. We target student accommodation, wellness stays and long-term residences for consistent visibility, qualified leads and stronger conversion.

Alliances & Partnerships

Strategic partnerships across hospitality, wellness, medical, academic and lifestyle sectors unlock direct consumer access, strengthen market intelligence and create unique value propositions — supporting diversification into high-growth segments and expanding distribution networks.
